"Dasvidaniya"

Oh boy. This, I suppose, was my first game. Unfortunately, our group's cohesion was poor, the idea "okay" at best, and the execution also poor. However, it was, more or less, the first time in which I took the lead in a college project out of necessity. Much of the game's content was, for the most part, developed by me (at the last minute, when I realized we had not completed the assignment) To be fair, I think it's better than I remember. The writing, okay. The visuals, fine. The actual content still was lacking, though. That being said, I beg you not to give it a shot. But if you must, here you go.
